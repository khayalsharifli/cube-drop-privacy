9. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using Android's standard SharedPreferences mechanism, which is sandboxed to the App and inaccessible to other apps. We employ no servers, databases, or cloud storage.

If your device is lost, stolen, or reset, your game progress will be lost as it exists only on the device. We recommend using Android's built-in backup features if you wish to preserve your scores.

10. Data Retention & Deletion

Game data (scores and preferences) is retained on your device as long as the App is installed. To delete all data:

  • Go to Android Settings → Apps → Cube Drop
  • Tap "Clear Data" — this erases all scores and preferences
  • Or simply uninstall the App
