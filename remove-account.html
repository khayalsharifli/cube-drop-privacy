Remove Account / Delete Data

Since Cube Drop: Brain Block Game does not require account creation, there is no account to delete. To erase all your game data, simply:

  • Go to Android Settings → Apps → Cube Drop: Brain Block Game → Storage
  • Tap Clear Data

Or simply uninstall the app.
